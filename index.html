About

Karsa Bintang

Karsa Bintang was born from the meeting point of art, intuition, and academia. With over 16 years of experience in divination, we have walked alongside countless seekers, reading them tarot, tracing their stars, and interpreting symbols into stories of clarity. Our background as an academic enriches this practice with structure and rigour, ensuring that every session is not only pedagogical but also intentional, grounded, and thoughtfully designed.

Karsa Bintang works with purpose-driven individuals who are committed to their own personal growth and professional growth through spiritual evolution. Through various divination modalities such as tarot, astrology, numerology, and I Ching, we empower you to uncover your unique path, allowing your authentic light to shine and enabling you to channel your gifts toward achieving your professional goals and making a meaningful impact on the world. Each reading is crafted to help you see beyond the surface of your questions, revealing patterns that shape your path and possibilities that await your becoming.
