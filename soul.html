About this space

A ceremonial encounter with the self you have always been.

There are questions that cannot be answered by strategy or by love. They are questions about the self — about what you are made of at the deepest level, what patterns have governed your life without your full awareness, and what you are being called to become in this chapter.

The Self-Introduction Rites exist for those who are ready to stop circling the edges of themselves and step inside. These are not readings. They are encounters. Each rite is a structured ritual of deep cartomantic practice — drawing on the full power of the Tarot to hold up a mirror that does not flatter, does not deceive, and does not look away.
