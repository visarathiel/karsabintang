What SOLUM actually is

SOLUM is a six-session private coaching container. Not a course. Not a group program. Not a reading. A sustained, rigorous, deeply personal engagement, using several modalities such as Tarot, Ba Zi, Hellenistic Astrology, Human Design, and the principles of Law of Assumption to help you build who you are becoming, not just understand who you have been.
